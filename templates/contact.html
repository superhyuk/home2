Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our AI-powered safety solutions.

How quickly can the system be deployed?

Deployment time depends on your facility size and complexity. Basic sensor installations take 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive AI monitoring systems may require 4-8 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

What kind of ongoing support do you provide?

Yes! We offer 24/7 monitoring services, regular system maintenance, AI model updates, and technical support to ensure your safety systems operate reliably.

What types of sensors do you support?

We support a wide range of industrial sensors including temperature, pressure, vibration, gas detection, humidity, and many others. Our platform is designed to integrate with most standard industrial IoT devices.

Can you integrate with existing safety systems?

Absolutely! We can integrate with existing SCADA systems, PLCs, and legacy safety equipment. Our solutions are designed to enhance your current infrastructure with AI capabilities.
